Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and maintain your account.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the expense tracking service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typography. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No personal or financial data is shared with Google as part of this.

We do not share your data with any other third parties, sell it to advertisers, or use third-party analytics services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
